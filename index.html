Hobby

Photography

Photography is a hobby I pursue because I enjoy observing my surroundings and capturing seemingly simple moments that tell a story. Photography has taught me to be more sensitive to details, atmosphere, and emotion within a frame, rather than simply taking a picture.

I often photograph human activity, the environment, and everyday life with a natural and simple approach. I don't pursue overly visual images, but rather focus on the impression and story I want to convey through the photo.

For me, photography isn't just a hobby; it's also the foundation for my multimedia work.
